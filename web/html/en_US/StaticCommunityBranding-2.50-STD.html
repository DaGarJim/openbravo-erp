Openbravo Exchange

Openbravo Exchange is the marketplace to find Openbravo extensions, connectors, localizations, solutions, and tools that can be easily installed directly from the browser.  Exchange provides cost-effective, quality packaged functionality that you "assemble to fit" to meet your business requirements.  Don't customize if you can extend instead!
